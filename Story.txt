Once upon a time, in a dense jungle, lived a monkey, who resided on a berry tree, which was on the bank of the river. In the same forest, there lived a crocodile and is wife.
One day, the crocodile came to the back of the river rested under a tree, and it just so happened that that was the same tree that the monkey lived. The kind hearted monkey offered him some fruits.
The crocodile came the next day for more fruits
